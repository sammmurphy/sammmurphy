Welcome to my website! My name is Samm Murphy and I am making this website to showcase the percentage of americans who prefer dogs to cats.
As someone who does not have a preference this is a very interesting topic that is very near and dear to my heart.

(https://www.statista.com/forecasts/978845/preference-of-dogs-vs-cats-in-the-us)
The webiste provided above showcases a pie chart of people who prefer dogs, cats, like them equally, and dislikes them equally.
However, if I were to conduct my.own study, I would not provide the last two options and make people pick a dog or cat to get a better data set for what I am interested in.
